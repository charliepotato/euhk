Charlie obtained his BA Degree with First-class Honours from The Education University of Hong Kong, one of the research-intensive universities for Teacher Education and Training. Charlie serves as a registered teacher in elementary sector, curator and university research assistant for the research projects and knowledge transfer funded projects.

He has explored creative works in several areas including philosophy, Buddhism, spatial thinking, and digital culture. Past creative works have been featured and exhibited in the United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, South Korea, and Hong Kong.

Charlie earned a Bachelor of Arts degree with first-class honors from The Education University of Hong Kong, a research-intensive institution renowned for its excellence in teacher education and training. Currently, Charlie serves as an in-service teacher in the elementary sector, a curator, digital learning speicialist in developing SCORM packages, and university research assistant for funded research and knowledge transfer projects.

Charlie has a diverse range of interests and has explored various fields including philosophy, Buddhism, spatial thinking, and digital culture. His creative works have been featured and exhibited in esteemed venues across the globe, including the United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, South Korea, and Hong Kong.
